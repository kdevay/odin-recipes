The Perfect Smoothie

Description

The title says it all--this is the best and last smoothie recipe you will ever need. The combination of ripe mango and tart raspberry are the real stars of this recipe. These, paired with the creamy greek yogurt and banana create a perfectly balanced flavor profile.

This is a well-balanced beverage, containing over 14g of protein and a whopping 125% of your daily recommended vitamin c!

This recipe can be prepared in 10 minutes, and serves two.



Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup dry oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 4 strawberries, stems removed
  • 1/4 cup raspberries
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 mango, chopped
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp agave sweetener

Steps

  1. Add oatmeal and orange juice to the blender, and blend on high until homogeneous.
  2. Add remaining ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend on high speed until smoothie is a consistent color and no lumps remain.
